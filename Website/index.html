Our History

Our Foundation

Founded in 2026 by Sam Menninga and Austin Rich of Team 935, Northstar was born out of a mission to revolutionize the scouting landscape within the FIRST community. Observing a widespread need for higher-quality, data-driven insights—both within our own program and across the competition as a whole—we identified a significant gap in existing tools. Traditional scouting solutions often lacked the flexibility and depth required to meet the evolving demands of modern FRC teams. We engineered Northstar to bridge this gap. By combining the advanced features our own team required with the intuitive functionality necessary for teams of all experience levels, we have created a comprehensive ecosystem designed to make scouting seamless, efficient, and accessible for everyone.

Key Pillars of Northstar

  • Innovation: Moving beyond static spreadsheets to dynamic, real-time data analysis tailored for the heat of competition.
  • Inclusivity: Developing a platform that empowers rookie teams and seasoned veterans alike, regardless of their technical background.
  • Efficiency: Streamlining the data collection process so teams can focus on high-level strategy rather than manual entry.
  • Built by Participants: As members of Team 935, we designed Northstar with a deep, first-hand understanding of the unique challenges faced on the field and in the pits.
