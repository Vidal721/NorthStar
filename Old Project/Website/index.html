FAQ

Common questions

What counts as an "event"? ▾
Any official FRC event — regional, district event, district championship, or World Championship. Off-season events also count against your event limit on the Starter plan.
Does Northstar work offline? ▾
Yes. The scouter app works fully offline and syncs automatically when a connection is restored. The admin dashboard needs a LAN connection to receive live data, which we handle over Bluetooth mesh or local Wi-Fi.
What devices do scouters use? ▾
Any modern phone or tablet with a browser. iOS, Android, Chromebook — all work. No app install required. We recommend tablets for the best experience on the form builder.
Can I export our data? ▾
Always. Every plan lets you export raw match data as CSV or JSON. Championship tier also gets API access for custom downstream tooling.
How does the equation engine work? ▾
You write simple expressions referencing field names from your scouting form — like `autoScore * 2 + teleopScore + (endgameClimb ? 5 : 0)`. The engine evaluates these for every team and auto-sorts the picklist.
Is there a student discount? ▾
The Starter plan is genuinely free. For Pro and Championship, we have grants available for teams with financial need — especially Title I schools. Just reach out to Team 935 directly.
